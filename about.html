About Me

Hi, I'm Othniel Solomon – a 2D motion designer who brings ideas to life through movement. I craft visually engaging stories using a mix of illustration, animation, and design. With tools like After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Figma, I blend creativity with precision to create animations that don’t just look good but feel right. Whether it’s an explainer video that simplifies a complex idea, a logo animation that adds personality to a brand, or a dynamic visual that captivates an audience, I make sure every frame has purpose. My work is about more than just motion—it's about connection, clarity, and impact.
